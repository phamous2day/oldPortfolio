Updating Library Computers

Upon visiting the school's library, I found all the computers were running Pentium 2 processors. A lot of teachers expressed frustrations with getting the computers launch applications successfully. I proposed installing a light-weight distribution of Linux as a way to salvage the computers' worth and adding more useful open-source software for students to use. Distributions of Edubuntu and Linux Mint LDE were used successfully helped improve computer efficiencies and time distributions to lesson plans.
